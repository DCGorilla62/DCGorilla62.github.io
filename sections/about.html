About

I am a PhD candidate in experimental astrophysics at The Ohio State University, where my work focuses on high-energy cosmic rays and the development of precision instrumentation for balloon-borne experiments. I grew up in the San Francisco Bay Area in an immigrant Latino family, and my academic path began at Chabot Community College before transferring to California State University, East Bay.

My journey has always been shaped by community. I have been fortunate to learn from mentors, programs, and peers who opened doors for me, including the APS Bridge Program, Cal-Bridge, and the APS National Mentoring Community.
